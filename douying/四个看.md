# look,see,watch和read的区别

## look

+ **强调集中注意力地去看的动作**

> She looked at me and smiled
> The student look at blackboard
>
## see

+ 强调看的结果；眼睛找到它了

>I can see the words on the wall

## watch

+ 指眼睛跟踪图像的移动以便于观察它的变化
  
>I ofter watch TV in the evening

## read

+ 含有阅读方法理解内容的意思

>My father likes to read newspapers
